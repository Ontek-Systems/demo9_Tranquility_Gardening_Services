Our Services

Everything Your Garden Deserves

From a single hedge to a full estate maintenance programme, we deliver exceptional results for homeowners, landlords, and businesses across Kent.

Expert Hedge Management

Precision trimming, sympathetic shaping, height and width reductions, and full removals. We specialise in traditional approaches — including careful beech hedge reductions — to improve light, access, and overall garden flow.

Perfect for

Overgrown boundaries, formal gardens, estate hedgerows, beech & box hedges

Precision Lawn Care

Professional lawn maintenance, seasonal treatments, and pristine mowing that transforms tired grass into a feature you're proud of. We enhance the visual appeal of both domestic and commercial landscapes.

Perfect for

Domestic gardens, commercial grounds, rental properties, country estates

Bespoke Planting & Plant Care

Tailored garden design, seasonal planting, expert pruning, and ongoing horticultural care to bring your outdoor vision to life. We nurture plant health to ensure your garden looks spectacular season after season.

Perfect for

New borders, seasonal colour schemes, shrub pruning, tree care

Fencing Installation & Repair

From sturdy boundary fencing to elegant decorative panels, we supply and install a wide range of fencing solutions for domestic and commercial properties across Kent. We also carry out repairs, replacements, and storm damage restoration.

Perfect for

Garden boundaries, driveways, paddocks, commercial perimeters, storm damage repairs

Contact Us
What areas of Kent do you cover?
We serve all of Kent, including Tunbridge Wells, Canterbury, Maidstone, Sevenoaks, Ashford, Folkestone, and surrounding villages and rural areas. If you're in Kent, we'd love to hear from you — just give Brad a call on +44 7943 909311.
Do you offer regular maintenance programmes or one-off visits?
Both! We offer flexible, tailored maintenance programmes for clients who want their gardens looked after on a regular fortnightly or monthly basis, as well as one-off visits for hedge trimming, seasonal clearances, planting, and more. We'll find an arrangement that works perfectly for you.
Do you work on commercial properties as well as domestic gardens?
Absolutely. We work with a wide range of clients including private homeowners, country estate managers, landlords with rental portfolios, and commercial businesses such as offices, retail units, and schools across Kent. We tailor our service to suit each property's specific needs.
Can you reduce an overgrown or neglected hedge safely?
Yes, this is one of our specialities. We take a sympathetic, traditional approach to hedge reductions — including beech hedges — ensuring the plant's long-term health is preserved while restoring proportion, improving light, and enhancing access. We'll always advise honestly on the best course of action before we start.
How do I get a quote?
Getting a quote is easy and completely free. Simply fill in the quick quote form on this page, give Brad a call on +44 7943 909311, or drop us an email at tranquilitygardenservices@gmail.com. We'll arrange a convenient time to visit your property and provide a clear, no-obligation quote.
What types of planting and garden design do you offer?
We offer bespoke planting schemes tailored to your garden's soil, aspect, and your personal preferences — from seasonal bedding and perennial borders to shrub planting, tree care, and expert pruning. Whether you have a clear vision or need guidance from scratch, we'll design something beautiful that thrives year after year.
Are you fully insured?
Yes, Tranquility Gardening Services is fully insured, giving you complete peace of mind whenever we're working on your property. We operate to professional standards at all times, whether on a small residential garden or a large commercial estate.
How quickly can you start?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within a few hours and can usually arrange an initial visit very quickly. Start dates depend on our current schedule, but we always do our best to accommodate urgent requests. The sooner you get in touch, the sooner we can get your garden looking its very best.
