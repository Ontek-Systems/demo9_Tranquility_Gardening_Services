Send Us a Message
How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within a few hours during normal working hours (Monday to Saturday, 8am–6pm). Brad personally handles all enquiries, so you can always expect a prompt and honest response. For urgent matters, calling +44 7943 909311 is the fastest way to reach us.
Is the quote genuinely free and no-obligation?
Absolutely. Every quote we provide is completely free and comes with zero obligation. We'll visit your property, assess your requirements, and give you honest, transparent pricing — there's no pressure and no hidden charges. You decide if and when you'd like to proceed.
What information should I include in my message?
The more detail you can provide, the better — but don't worry if you're not sure exactly what you need. It helps to mention the type of work you're looking for (e.g. hedge trimming, lawn care, regular maintenance), a rough description of your garden or grounds, your location in Kent, and whether you'd prefer a one-off visit or ongoing service. Brad will ask any follow-up questions he needs during his site visit.
Do you take on both small domestic gardens and large estates?
Yes, we work across the full spectrum — from compact residential gardens requiring a regular tidy-up to large country estates needing comprehensive, ongoing grounds management. We also serve commercial clients including offices, schools, and retail premises. No job is too small or too large; we tailor our service entirely to your needs.
What happens after I submit the form?
Once you submit your enquiry, Brad will review your details and get back to you personally — usually within a few hours. He'll arrange a convenient time to visit your property anywhere in Kent, take a look at what's needed, and provide you with a clear, itemised quote at no cost. From there, it's entirely up to you whether you'd like to go ahead.
Can I contact you directly by phone instead?
Of course! We're always happy to have a chat. You can call Brad directly on +44 7943 909311 or email tranquilitygardenservices@gmail.com at any time. Phone is often the quickest way to discuss your requirements and get a same-day response, especially for more complex or urgent projects.
